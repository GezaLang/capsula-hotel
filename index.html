Our Inspiration

Hop INN is inspired by the efficiency of Japanese capsule hotels and the dynamic business atmosphere of Ingolstadt.

We believe modern travel should be flexible, affordable and smart. Whether you're arriving late by train, taking a break from a long drive, or visiting for business - you deserve a real bed, a refreshing shower, and a calm, private space.

At the same time, our foyer and co-working lounge create space for connection and exchange. Meet fellow travelers, students, professionals and explorers - if you want to.

Hop INN combines all: privacy and community, comfort and simplicity & technology and hospitality.
