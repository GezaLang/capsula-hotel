Privacy & Security: Our hotel is designed for peace of mind

Your Safety Comes First

At Hop Ingo, privacy and security are not extras: they are our foundation!

  • Individual QR-code access: only you can enter your capsule
  • No direct visibility from outside: privacy glass + curtain layer
  • 24/7 monitored public areas (never inside capsules)
  • Lockable personal storage inside every capsule
  • Sound-insulated walls for maximum discretion
  • ZERO tolerance for any unauthorized access or disturbances
  • Safe environment especially designed for solo female travelers

No matter whether you are arriving late at night or staying alone, you can feel protected, undisturbed and fully in control.

Foyer & Lounge

Our Inspiration

Hop Ingo is inspired by the efficiency of Japanese capsule hotels and the dynamic business atmosphere of Ingolstadt.

We believe modern travel should be flexible, affordable and smart. Whether you're arriving late by train, taking a break from a long drive, or visiting for business - you deserve a real bed, a refreshing shower, and a calm, private space.

At the same time, our foyer and co-working lounge create space for connection and exchange. Meet fellow travelers, students, professionals and explorers - if you want to.

Hop Ingo combines all: privacy and community, comfort and simplicity & technology and hospitality.
